About Mumbai

Mumbai, the city of dreams inspires every resident to dream big and work for it. It is one of the largest cities in India, which is considered as the financial, commercial and entertainment capital of India. It is the largest port of foreign trade. It is the hub of cinema being the center of the Bollywood Film Industry, one of the biggest film industries in the world. Mumbai consists of two regions namely, Mumbai City District and Mumbai Suburban District, covering a total of around 603.4 sq km. the Mumbai. It is situated at the mouth of the Ulhas River on the western coast Konkan and bounded by the Arabian Sea to the west. Mumbai was once an archipelago of seven islands, which have been inhabited since the South Asian Stone Age. In the third century BCE, the islands formed a part of the Kingdom of Ashoka.
