About Shimla

Shimla is a pleasing hill town located at 2200 m above mean sea level, in the state of Himachal Pradesh. Shimla is a famous summer retreat since the colonial era. In the 18th century, Shimla was a hidden hill region flourishing with high-altitude forests. The prime beauty of the hill town comes from the weather. While the plains get scorched up by the sun, this hill town gets a pleasing summer and a snow-drenched winter. The local cuisine of the land is Pahari dishes. However, today, the land has a fusion cuisine to satisfy the tourist population of the land. Apart from cuisine tasting, top activities to enjoy here are trekking, hiking, forest camping, river rafting, paragliding, ice skating, and others.Best time to visit in Shimla is from the month of March to June.
